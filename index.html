AKAN NAMES GENERATOR.


Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. This application will generate your AKAN name based on the day of the week you were born.



This Page prompts you to enter your year of birth, Month, date and your gender. Once you enter the correct date in the right format, you will get to know your Akan name.
